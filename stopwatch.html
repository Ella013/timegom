About TimeGom Stopwatch

TimeGom Stopwatch is an online stopwatch service that enables fast and accurate time measurement. Through the lap time recording function, you can easily measure and manage elapsed time in various situations such as exercise, experiments, and work.

Simple operations allow for start, pause, record, and reset, and it is optimized for both PC and mobile environments. Recorded lap times can be checked at a glance, helping with efficient time management.
